Spider Man

Spider-Man is a superhero in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.
Created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko,
he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the Silver Age of Comic Books.

Comics of spider man

The number of Spider-Man comics varies depending on which list you consult,
but it is estimated that there are around 613 Spider-Man comics published between 1963 and 2018.
